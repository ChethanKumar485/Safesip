How the simulator works

This simulates SafeSip's electrochemical biosensor response. In real-world use, the device dips into your drink, runs a 400ms voltammetric sweep, and classifies the result using on-device AI — all in under 0.8 seconds. This demo replicates the timing and alert behavior faithfully.
