THE SCIENCE

Electrochemistry Meets Machine Learning

Electrochemical Biosensors

Three working electrodes (carbon, gold, silver/silver-chloride) generate unique voltammetric signatures for each compound class. Cyclic voltammetry sweeps –0.6V to +1.2V in 400ms.

Edge-AI Classification

A 4-layer neural network (trained on 120,000 spectral samples) classifies the signature in <180ms. Runs entirely on-chip — no Wi-Fi or cellular required for detection.

Bluetooth 5.3 LE Alerts

When a threat is confirmed, the companion app triggers an SMS, push notification, and optional 911 call with a single tap — or automatically if manual override isn't used within 30 seconds.

OTA Substance Updates

New drugs emerge constantly. SafeSip's firmware updates over-the-air monthly, adding new compounds without replacing the hardware.
